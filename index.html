SCHOLARSHIP CRITERIA

At THF, we believe that education should be accessible to everyone regardless of financial constraints. As such, we offer need-based scholarships to eligible students who have successfully completed the admission and interview process. After admission, students may apply for the scholarship and will be assessed based on their financial need in class.

At THF, each course has a duration of six months, and admissions are open every six months, typically in June-July and December-January; therefore, interested candidates should apply now to their relevant institute.
